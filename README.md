# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is an interactive E-Commerce Sales Dashboard built using Google Looker Studio. The dashboard provides valuable business insights through data visualization, KPI cards, filtering options, and sales forecasting.

The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance across different categories, markets, countries, states, shipping modes, and time periods while predicting future sales trends.

## 🎯 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Shipping Cost

### Interactive Filters
- Year Filter
- Month Filter
- Market Filter
- Category Filter

### Visualizations
1. Sales by Category (Stacked Column Chart)
2. Sales by Market (Pie Chart)
3. Sales by Ship Mode (Funnel Chart)
4. Top 5 Sales by Country (Donut Chart)
5. Top 5 Sales by State (Tree Map)
6. Sales by Year (Line Chart)
7. Total Quantity Sold (Year/Month/Date Wise Line Chart)
8. Total Sales by Year (Line Chart)
9. One Month Sales Forecast (Forecasting Chart)

## 📊 Key Insights
- Technology category generated the highest sales.
- US market contributed the largest share of revenue.
- Standard Class was the most preferred shipping mode.
- California and New York were among the top-performing states.
- Sales showed a positive growth trend over the years.
- Forecasting indicates continued sales growth in the upcoming month.

## 📈 Forecasting Method
Sales forecasting was performed using Python by:
1. Aggregating monthly sales data.
2. Converting order dates into a time series.
3. Training a Linear Regression model.
4. Predicting sales for the next month.
5. Visualizing actual vs forecasted sales.
